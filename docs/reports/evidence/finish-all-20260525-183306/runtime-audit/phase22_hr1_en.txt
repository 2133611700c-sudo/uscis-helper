SIGNATURE ON THE PRINTED PACKET (MAIL FILING ONLY)

I-821 — Part 8 on page 10. Black or blue ink.

We do NOT sign for you. You must sign by hand after printing. This does not apply to document translations.

⚠ Mail filing only (from July 10, 2026): USCIS may DENY the printed application and KEEP YOUR FILING FEE if the signature on forms I-821/I-765 is invalid — copied image, typed name, or software-generated. This rule does not apply to document translations. Sign by hand in ink only.

USCIS GOVERNMENT FEE

Form I-912 (fee waiver) covers standard USCIS base fees only: I-821, biometrics, I-765.

⚠ Fees required by H.R.1 (effective May 29, 2026) CANNOT be waived via I-912 — they are non-waivable by statute. Verify current fees before mailing.

Verify fees: uscis.gov/feecalculator ↗
