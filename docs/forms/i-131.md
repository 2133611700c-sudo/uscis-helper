# Form I-131 Intelligence

Updated: 2026-04-30

- Official page: https://www.uscis.gov/i-131
- Form PDF: https://www.uscis.gov/sites/default/files/document/forms/i-131.pdf
- Instructions PDF: https://www.uscis.gov/sites/default/files/document/forms/i-131instr.pdf
- Edition date: 01/20/25
- Filing method: online for certain application types; otherwise by mail

## Official Use

USCIS states Form I-131 is used for travel documents, parole documents, and arrival/departure records.

USCIS also states eligible Ukrainian citizens and their immediate family members may apply for re-parole and refers them to Form I-131.

## Extractable from documents

- passport name
- date of birth
- passport number
- passport issue/expiration
- country of citizenship
- I-94 number
- parole expiration date if visible
- A-number if visible on USCIS notice/EAD
- receipt number if visible on USCIS notice

## Manual user inputs

- current address
- mailing address
- phone
- email
- height
- weight
- eye color
- hair color
- travel history details
- immigration history details
- USCIS online account number if any
- any field not visible on uploaded documents
